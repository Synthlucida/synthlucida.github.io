HOW TO DRAW

DRAWING

Press and drag with your mouse or finger to paint glowing light trails. A single tap leaves a dot.

BRUSH

Pick a color from the palette and set the size with the slider. The "+" swatch opens a full custom color picker. Your last choice is remembered next time you visit.

TOOLS

  • 🧹 ERASER — erase instead of draw
  • ✨ SPARKLE — turn the glowing trail on or off
  • ↺ UNDO — step back one stroke
  • CLEAR — wipe the canvas (still undoable)
  • ▶ ANIMATE — replay your drawing stroke by stroke, in the order you drew it
  • ⬇ VIDEO — record the animated replay and download it as a WEBM video
  • ⬇ SAVE IMAGE — download your drawing as a PNG

BRING THE PICTURE TO LIFE

Draw a picture (e.g. a bird), then press ➕ MARK PART and drag over a part you want to move (e.g. a wing). Choose a motion — FLAP (flapping), SWAY (gentle swaying), SPIN (rotation), or BOB (bouncing). Add as many parts as you like (both wings, a tail...) and hit 🦋 LIVE — the rest of the picture stays still while the marked parts move around the point where they're "attached" to the body.

THEMES

Pick a THEME from the row above the canvas to change the background mood. It doesn't change your brush color.
