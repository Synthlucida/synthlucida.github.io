HOW TO DRAW

DRAWING

Press and drag with your mouse or finger to paint glowing light trails. A single tap leaves a dot.

BRUSH

Pick a color from the palette and set the size with the slider. Your last choice is remembered next time you visit.

TOOLS

  • 🧹 ERASER — erase instead of draw
  • ✨ SPARKLE — turn the glowing trail on or off
  • ↺ UNDO — step back one stroke
  • CLEAR — wipe the canvas (still undoable)
  • ⬇ SAVE IMAGE — download your drawing as a PNG

THEMES

Pick a THEME from the row above the canvas to change the background mood. It doesn't change your brush color.
