HOW TO PLAY

GOAL

Clear horizontal lines on the grid. Every line you clear adds to your permanent Career Lines total — that's what unlocks new SYNTHLUCIDA ambient tracks.

CONTROLS — DESKTOP

  • ← → : Move left / right
  • ↑ : Rotate
  • ↓ : Soft drop

CONTROLS — MOBILE

  • ◀ ▶ : Move left / right
  • ↻ : Rotate
  • ▼ : Soft drop (hold for continuous)

UNLOCKING TRACKS

Switch between ASMR / PIANO / CHILL categories using the buttons above the board. Each track needs a set number of Career Lines to unlock. Once it shows ✓ READY, tap it to play.

BONUS ROUND & BOMB

Every 20 Career Lines earns a 💣 BOMB charge and triggers a 60-second Bonus Round with 2x score. Use BOMB to drop a special piece that instantly clears the bottom 5 rows.

LEVELS & SCORE

Clearing several lines at once scores extra points. Every 10 lines in a run raises your Level and speeds up the falling blocks.

OTHER CONTROLS

SAVE stores your progress manually (it also autosaves). RESIZE toggles fullscreen. PAUSE pauses the game. Pick a visual THEME from the row above the board. Career Lines are permanent and are never lost, even after Game Over.
