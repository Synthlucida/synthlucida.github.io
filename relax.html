Information & Guide

⚠ EPILEPSY WARNING: This application contains gently pulsating visual elements (twinkling stars, light orbs and other). If you suffer from photosensitive epilepsy, please use caution.

How to use the app

SYNTHLUCIDA is an interactive ambient canvas designed for relaxation and creative flow. Headphones are highly recommended.

  • Binaural Beats: Clicking "BINAURAL" activates low-frequency waves that help the brain transition into a state of deep relaxation.
  • Nature: The "NATURE" button triggers a loop of night nature sounds.
  • Tap Synthesizer: Touching anywhere on the night sky plays the generative synthesizer. X position determines the pitch, Y position controls filter/tone.

Sound Customization (🎛)

Opening the synthesizer panel gives you control over your own sound for beginners and experts alike:

  • Wave: Choose basic waveform (Sine, Triangle, Square, Saw).
  • Unison: Adds detuned duplicate voices for a huge, wide sound.
  • Reverb: Controls the depth of spatial hall effect.
  • Noise: Blends in subtle background noise for texture.
  • Presets: Use the dropdown and SAVE button to keep your favorite setup.
