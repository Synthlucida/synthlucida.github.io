How to use SYNTHLUCIDA RELAX

SYNTHLUCIDA RELAX is an interactive ambient canvas designed for your relaxation and creative flow. Here is a complete guide on how to get the most out of the application:

  • BINAURAL WAVES (BINAURAL): Switch between Delta (2Hz for deep sleep), Theta (5Hz for meditation), or Alpha (9Hz for relaxation) frequencies. These waves synchronize brain hemispheres. (Headphones are required for the correct effect!)
  • NATURE SOUNDS (NATURE): Activate a smooth, relaxing night nature and cricket loop that beautifully underscores your musical experience.
  • TAP SYNTHESIZER (TAP SYNTH): Click or tap anywhere on the night sky. This triggers a generative synthesizer tone. The higher up on the screen you click, the higher the pitch. Additionally, a visual star cluster appears around the touch point.
  • SEQUENCER (KICK & MELODY): Open the 🎛 panel. Design a sound in the panel below, then hit + ADD SOUND to turn it into its own 32-step track. Design another sound and add it too — each track keeps its own timbre, and they all play together, so you can layer multiple different sounds into one track. Each track can be muted (MUTE) or removed (✕). KICK SEQ adds a separate rhythmic kick drum. Use the PLAY button to start, and the BPM slider to set the speed.
  • How to program a melody into a track: The sequencer remembers the exact tone you last played. First, tap anywhere on the night sky to play and "grab" a specific tone. Then click a square in that track's grid to lock that exact tone into that step. To place a different tone in another step (even in the same track), tap a new spot in the sky first, then click the target square.
  • SOUND DESIGN (PANEL 🎛): As a creator, you can go wild here. Change musical scales (Scale), oscillator wave shapes (OSC1/OSC2), adjust filter, decay, reverb (space), echo (delay), or add saturation (Drive). Reverb, delay (SYNC DEL.) and echo each have their own independent ON/OFF button right next to the slider, so you can instantly cut that effect without losing the level you dialed in — turning one off never affects the other sounds or effects still playing. Use the SAVE button to save your preset to your browser.
  • SLEEP TIMER (TIMER): Set an automatic shutdown for 15, 30, or 60 minutes. Once the countdown ends, all sounds will gently turn off automatically so you can sleep in peace.
  • RECORDING (REC 🔴): Want to save your jam? Click REC to start recording. After clicking STOP again, the session will automatically download to your device as a high-quality .wav file.
  • Kick Drum: Click the squares in the KICK SEQ grid to build your rhythm. Adjust the BPM slider to set your global tempo.
  • SAVE: Saves your current sequencer patterns and all sound design parameters to your browser so you can pick up exactly where you left off.
  • Preset: A dropdown menu that lets you switch between the factory 'Default' state and your saved 'User' patch.
  • Reset: Removes all sequencer tracks, resets all synth parameters to their default state, and clears the looper buffer to give you a completely blank canvas.
  • LIVE DAY/NIGHT SKY: The sky is synced to your device's real clock, cycling gently through night, dawn, day and dusk as time actually passes — the horizon glow, star brightness and moon all shift along with it. This uses only your local clock time, never your location: no GPS, no permission prompt, and nothing is sent anywhere.
  • MOON: The moon is rendered from a real photograph and its lit/dark phase updates automatically to match today's actual lunar phase.
  • NIGHT SKY DETAILS: Beyond the stars themselves, keep an eye out for the small extra touches: gentle random twinkling and occasional brighter flares, shooting stars that streak across every so often, satellites drifting slowly overhead, and a handful of real constellations (including the Big Dipper, Cassiopeia and Orion) woven into the starfield.
