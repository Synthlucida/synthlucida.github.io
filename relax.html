How to use SYNTHLUCIDA RELAX

SYNTHLUCIDA RELAX is an interactive ambient canvas designed for your relaxation and creative flow. Here is a complete guide on how to get the most out of the application:

  • BINAURAL WAVES (BINAURAL): Switch between Delta (2Hz for deep sleep), Theta (5Hz for meditation), or Alpha (9Hz for relaxation) frequencies. These waves synchronize brain hemispheres. (Headphones are required for the correct effect!)
  • NATURE SOUNDS (NATURE): Activate a smooth, relaxing night nature and cricket loop that beautifully underscores your musical experience.
  • TAP SYNTHESIZER (TAP SYNTH): Click or tap anywhere on the night sky. This triggers a generative synthesizer tone. The higher up on the screen you click, the higher the pitch. Additionally, a visual star cluster appears around the touch point.
  • SEQUENCER (KICK & MELODY): Open the 🎛 panel. Here you will find two 32-step sequencers (for two musical bars). MELODY SEQ plays tones from your Tap Synth, KICK SEQ adds a rhythmic kick drum. Use the PLAY button to start, and the BPM slider to set the speed.
  • SOUND DESIGN (PANEL 🎛): As a creator, you can go wild here. Change musical scales (Scale), oscillator wave shapes (OSC1/OSC2), adjust filter, decay, reverb (space), echo (delay), or add saturation (Drive). Use the SAVE button to save your preset to your browser.
  • SLEEP TIMER (TIMER): Set an automatic shutdown for 15, 30, or 60 minutes. Once the countdown ends, all sounds will gently turn off automatically so you can sleep in peace.
  • RECORDING (REC 🔴): Want to save your jam? Click REC to start recording. After clicking STOP again, the session will automatically download to your device as a high-quality .wav file.
  • Melody & Kick Sequencer: Open the 🎛 panel to program up to 32 steps of ambient melodies and rhythmic kick drums.
  • How to program a melody: The sequencer remembers the exact tone you played last. First, tap anywhere on the night sky to play and "grab" a specific tone. Then, click a square in the MELODY SEQ grid to lock that exact tone into that step. To add a different tone to another step, you must first tap a new spot in the sky, and then click the target square in the sequencer.
  • Melody & Kick Sequencer: Open the 🎛 panel to program up to 32 steps of ambient melodies and rhythmic kick drums.
  • Kick Drum: Click the squares in the KICK SEQ grid to build your rhythm. Adjust the BPM slider to set your global tempo.
  • SAVE: Saves your current sequencer patterns and all sound design parameters to your browser so you can pick up exactly where you left off.
  • Preset: A dropdown menu that lets you switch between the factory 'Default' state and your saved 'User' patch.
  • Reset: Clears both sequencer grids, resets all synth parameters to their default state, and clears the looper buffer to give you a completely blank canvas.
